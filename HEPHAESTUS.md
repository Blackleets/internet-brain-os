# HEPHAESTUS

## The Intelligence Forge

Hephaestus is the project identity and architectural metaphor for the system formerly known as Internet Brain OS.

### The Forge
The system transforms raw public information into structured intelligence.

### The Anvil
The Kernel provides durable primitives: cases, evidence, entities, relationships, memory, validation, and reports.

### The Fire
Agents and models provide reasoning, extraction, synthesis, and specialized intelligence.

### The Knowledge Graph
The system connects facts and entities into a navigable model of the world.

### The Memory
Every valuable investigation should leave the system smarter than before.

### The Output
The result is not more data. The result is better understanding and better decisions.

> Raw information enters the forge. Intelligence comes out.
